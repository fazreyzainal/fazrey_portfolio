Quadruped (Unitree Go2) Autonomous Navigation

ROS 2 Nav2 + SLAM Toolbox indoor autonomy with safe teleop handover, route tuning, and public-safe behaviors.

What is the project?

Implemented reliable indoor navigation on the Unitree Go2 by bringing up ROS 2 Nav2 with SLAM Toolbox, tuning planners/costmaps for corridors and booths, and integrating a low-latency teleop path for safe handover.

Challenges

  • Localization drift and re-localization near feature-poor corridors.
  • Planner oscillations at tight turns and near dynamic obstacles.
  • Safe manual‚áÑauto switching for public demos; watchdogs & soft E-stop.

How I overcame them

  • Used SLAM Toolbox with loop-closure params; map cleanup + re-localization poses.
  • Tuned costmap inflation, voxel layers, and DWB critics; added approach/stop zones.
  • Added watchdog timers, speed caps, and a mode arbiter for clean teleop/autonomy handover.

Tech

ROS 2 (Nav2, SLAM Toolbox), Python/C++, OpenCV, MQTT/WebRTC, Linux/Jetson, Git/GitHub.
